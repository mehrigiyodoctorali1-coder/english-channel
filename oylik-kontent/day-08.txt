⏰ 09:00 — POST 1
🗣 Part 2: Cue card (1-2 daqiqa gapirish)

Sizga karta (cue card) beriladi: mavzu + 3-4 yo'naltiruvchi savol. 1 daqiqa tayyorgarlik (yozish mumkin), keyin 1-2 daqiqa to'xtamasdan gapirasiz.

Maslahat: tayyorgarlik daqiqasida har bandga 2-3 ta KALIT SO'Z yozing — to'liq jumla emas.

📚 Manba: IELTS.org — Speaking Part 2 format

⏰ 12:00 — POST 2
💬 Vaqtni cho'zish iboralari

Gapni davom ettirish uchun:
• What I'd like to talk about is ... — Men gapirmoqchi bo'lgan narsa ...
• The reason I chose this is ... — Buni tanlash sababim ...
• It reminds me of ... — Bu menga ... ni eslatadi
• Looking back, ... — Orqaga nazar tashlasam, ...

Bu iboralar fikrlash uchun vaqt beradi.

⏰ 15:00 — POST 3
🎯 Misol cue card

"Describe a person who inspired you."
You should say: who they are / how you know them / what they did / why they inspired you.

Struktura: 1) Kim → 2) Qachondan tanish → 3) Nima qildi → 4) Nega ilhomlantirdi (eng ko'p shu yerga vaqt).
Hikoya tarzida gapiring — bu tabiiy ravonlik beradi.
