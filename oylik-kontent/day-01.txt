⏰ 09:00 — POST 1
🗣 Speaking Part 1: tabiiy javob bering

IELTS Speaking 4 mezon bo'yicha baholanadi (har biri 25%): Fluency & Coherence, Lexical Resource, Grammatical Range, Pronunciation.

Part 1'da imtihonchi uy, ish, hobbi haqida oddiy savol beradi. Asosiy maslahat: hech qachon bir so'z bilan javob bermang — javobni sabab yoki misol bilan kengaytiring (extend).

❓ "Do you like cooking?"
✅ "Yes, I really enjoy it, especially at weekends, because it helps me relax."

⏰ 12:00 — POST 2
💬 Useful phrases: javobni kengaytirish

"PEE" usuli — Point (fikr) + Explain (sabab) + Example (misol):
• To be honest, ... — Rostini aytsam, ...
• ... , especially when ... — ayniqsa ... bo'lganda
• For instance, ... — Masalan, ...
• It depends on ... — ... ga bog'liq

Bu iboralar fikrni tabiiy bog'laydi (cohesion).

⏰ 15:00 — POST 3
🎯 Worked example: "Where are you from?"

❌ Zaif: "From Kokand."
✅ Kuchli (B2): "I'm from Kokand, a historic city in the Fergana Valley. It's famous for its old bazaars, and I've lived there my whole life, so I know it really well."

E'tibor bering: joy + tavsif + shaxsiy bog'lanish. 2–3 jumla yetarli.

📝 Homework + Useful word

Bugun 3 savolga ovozli javob bering (telefon diktofoniga yozing), har biri kamida 2 jumla:
1. Do you work or study?
2. What do you do in your free time?
3. Do you prefer mornings or evenings?
