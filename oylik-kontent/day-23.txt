✍️ Opinion essay (Agree/Disagree)

"To what extent do you agree or disagree?" — eng ko'p uchraydigan Task 2 turi.

Qoida: pozitsiyangiz AYDIN bo'lsin. Introduction'da pozitsiyani ayting va butun inshoda unga sodiq qoling.

Struktura: Intro (pozitsiya) → 2 ta sabab paragraf → Conclusion.

📚 Manba: British Council IELTS — Writing Task 2

⏰ 12:00 — POST 2
💬 Pozitsiya iboralari

• I completely agree that ... — To'liq qo'shilaman ...
• I partly agree, but ... — Qisman qo'shilaman, lekin ...
• In my view, the benefits outweigh the drawbacks — Menimcha, foydasi zararidan ko'p
• While some believe ..., I would argue ... — Ba'zilar ... deb hisoblasa-da, men ...

⏰ 15:00 — POST 3
🎯 Argumentni rivojlantirish (PEEL)

Har body paragraf:
• Point — asosiy fikr (topic sentence)
• Explain — nega? tushuntiring
• Example — misol keltiring
• Link — fikrni mavzuga bog'lang

Misol: "Firstly, technology saves time. By automating tasks, workers can focus on creative work. For instance, software now handles accounting. This boosts productivity."

⏰ 18:00 — POST 4
📝 Homework + Useful word

Mavzu: "Some people think exams are the best way to assess students. Do you agree?"
Faqat 1 ta BODY paragraf yozing (PEEL: Point + Explain + Example + Link).
