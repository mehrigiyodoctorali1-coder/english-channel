🔧 Relative clauses

2 ta gapni 1 ta qilib bog'laydi va so'z boyligini ko'rsatadi:
• who — odam uchun: "The man who called you is here."
• which — narsa uchun: "The book which I read was great."
• that — odam/narsa (gaplashuvда): "the car that I bought"
• where — joy uchun: "the city where I was born"

⏰ 12:00 — POST 2
💬 Defining vs Non-defining

• Defining — muhim ma'lumot, vergulsiz:
   "Students who study hard pass." (qaysi talabalar?)
• Non-defining — qo'shimcha ma'lumot, VERGUL bilan:
   "My brother, who lives in London, is a doctor."

Non-defining'da "that" ISHLATILMAYDI.

Relative clauses Writing va Speaking'da grammatik darajani (Grammatical Range) oshiradi:
❌ Oddiy: "I have a friend. He speaks five languages."
✅ B2: "I have a friend who speaks five languages."

📝 Homework + Useful word

2 gapni 1 ta qiling (who/which/where):
1. "This is the school. I studied there." →
2. "She has a car. It is electric." →
(Javob: 1 This is the school where I studied. 2 She has a car which is electric.)
