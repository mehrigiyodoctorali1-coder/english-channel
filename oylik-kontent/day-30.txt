⏰ 09:00 — POST 1
✍️ Eng ko'p uchraydigan xatolar

IELTS Writing'da B2 o'quvchilar ko'p qiladigan xatolar:
• Artikl: "I have car" → "I have A car"
• Birlik/ko'plik: "many informations" → "much information"
• Zamon: gap o'rtasida zamonни almashtirib yuborish
• So'z tartibi: "I like very much it" → "I like it very much"

⏰ 12:00 — POST 2
💬 Sanab bo'lmaydigan otlar (uncountable)

Bular ALWAYS birlik, "a/many" olmaydi:
• information, advice, news, knowledge, research, furniture, money, traffic
❌ "an advice / many advices"
✅ "some advice / a piece of advice"

⏰ 15:00 — POST 3
🎯 Tahrir (editing) — oxirgi 3 daqiqa

Yozib bo'lgach, tekshiring:
1. Har gapda fe'l (verb) bormi?
2. Artikllar (a/an/the) joyidami?
3. Birlik/ko'plik to'g'rimi (-s)?
4. So'z sonini sanang (Task 2 ≥ 250, Task 1 ≥ 150).

Tahrir 1 band ko'tarishi mumkin!

📝 Homework + Useful word
