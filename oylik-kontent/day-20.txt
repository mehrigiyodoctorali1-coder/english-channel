🔧 Passive Voice nima?

Harakat KIM tomonidan emas, NIMA bo'lganligi muhim bo'lganda ishlatiladi.
• Active: "They built the bridge in 1990."
• Passive: "The bridge was built in 1990."

Formula: be + V3 (past participle).
IELTS Writing Task 1 va rasmiy yozuvда juda foydali.

⏰ 12:00 — POST 2
💬 Zamonlarda passive

• Present: is/are + V3 → "English is spoken here."
• Past: was/were + V3 → "It was made in Japan."
• Present Perfect: has/have been + V3 → "The work has been done."
• Modal: can/will be + V3 → "It can be improved."

⏰ 15:00 — POST 3
🎯 Qachon ishlatish kerak?

• Harakat bajaruvchisi noma'lum yoki muhim emas: "My bike was stolen."
• Jarayon (process) tasvirlash: "The beans are roasted, then ground."
• Rasmiy/akademik uslub: "It is believed that ..."

⚠️ Haddan tashqari ishlatmang — Speaking'da active ko'proq tabiiy.
