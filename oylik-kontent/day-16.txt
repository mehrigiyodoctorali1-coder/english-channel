✍️ Coherence & Cohesion nima?

Bu Writing'ning 25% baliga teng mezon.
• Coherence — fikrlar mantiqiy tartibda joylashishi.
• Cohesion — fikrlarni bog'lovchi so'zlar (linking words) bilan ulash.

Yaxshi struktura + to'g'ri bog'lovchilar = examiner matnni oson o'qiydi = yuqori ball.

⏰ 12:00 — POST 2
💬 Linking words (bog'lovchilar)

• Qo'shish: moreover, in addition, furthermore
• Qarama-qarshilik: however, on the other hand, nevertheless
• Sabab: because, since, due to
• Natija: therefore, as a result, consequently
• Misol: for example, for instance, such as

⏰ 15:00 — POST 3
🎯 Maslahat: o'lchovni saqlang

❌ Har jumlada bog'lovchi = sun'iy va og'ir.
✅ Har paragrafda 2-3 ta tabiiy bog'lovchi yetarli.

Pronoun bilan ham bog'lang: "Pollution is rising. THIS affects health." ("this" — takrorni kamaytiradi).

Har paragraf bitta asosiy fikr (topic sentence) bilan boshlansin.

⏰ 18:00 — POST 4
📝 Homework + Useful word

2 jumlani 1 ta bog'lovchi bilan ulang:
"Public transport is cheap. It reduces traffic."
Misol: "Public transport is cheap; moreover, it reduces traffic."

💡 Useful word: however /haʊˈev.ər/ — biroq, lekin (qarama-qarshilikni ko'rsatadi)
