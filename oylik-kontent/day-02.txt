⏰ 09:00 — POST 1
✍️ Task 2: 4 paragrafli struktura

Eng ishonchli tuzilma — 4 paragraf:
1) Introduction — savolni o'z so'zingiz bilan qayta yozing (paraphrase) + pozitsiyangiz
2) Body 1 — birinchi asosiy fikr + tushuntirish + misol
3) Body 2 — ikkinchi asosiy fikr + tushuntirish + misol
4) Conclusion — fikrlarni qisqa xulosa qiling

2 ta yaxshi ishlangan paragraf 3 ta yarim paragrafdan yuqori baho oladi.

⏰ 12:00 — POST 2
💬 Introduction uchun foydali iboralar

Savolni qayta yozish (paraphrase) — bir xil so'zlarni takrorlamang:
• It is often argued that ... — Ko'pincha aytiladiki, ...
• In my opinion, ... — Mening fikrimcha, ...
• This essay will discuss ... — (BUNDAN QOCHING, kuch sarflamaydi)

Eslatma: "In today's modern world" kabi yodlangan iboralar ball qo'shmaydi.

⏰ 15:00 — POST 3
🎯 Misol: savolni paraphrase qilish

❓ Savol: "Some people think students should study abroad. Do you agree?"
✅ Intro: "Many believe that completing part of one's education in another country brings real benefits. I largely agree, and this essay will explain why."

E'tibor: "study abroad" → "completing education in another country".

⏰ 18:00 — POST 4
📝 Homework + Useful word

Quyidagi mavzuda faqat INTRODUCTION (2 jumla) yozing:
"Many people prefer to work from home. Is this a positive or negative development?"
Savolni o'z so'zingiz bilan qayta yozing + pozitsiyangizni bering.
