⏰ 12:00 — POST 2
💬 Tendensiya (trend) iboralari

O'sish: increase, rise, grow, climb, surge
Kamayish: decrease, fall, decline, drop, plummet
Barqaror: remain stable, stay constant, level off
Tezlik: sharply (keskin), gradually (asta-sekin), slightly (biroz)

Misol: "Sales rose sharply between 2020 and 2022."

⏰ 15:00 — POST 3
🎯 Overview — eng muhim qism

Overview'da RAQAM yozmang — faqat umumiy manzara:
✅ "Overall, the number of users increased over the period, while costs fell."
Examiner avval Overview'ni qidiradi. Yaxshi overview = yuqori ball (Task Achievement).

Solishtirish uchun: while, whereas, compared to, in contrast.
