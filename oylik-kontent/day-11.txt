⏰ 09:00 — POST 1
🎧 Multiple choice: to'g'ri javobni tanlash

3 ta variant (A, B, C) beriladi, bittasi to'g'ri. Qiyinligi: barcha variantlar audioda eshitiladi, lekin faqat bittasi savolga to'liq javob beradi.

Maslahat: audio boshlanishidan oldin savol va variantlarni o'qib oling (kalit so'zlarni belgilang).

📚 Manba: British Council IELTS — Listening tips

⏰ 12:00 — POST 2
💬 "Tuzoq"lardan ehtiyot bo'ling

So'zlovchi ko'pincha noto'g'ri variantni aytib, KEYIN tuzatadi:
"I was going to take the bus, but actually I decided to walk."
→ Javob: walk (bus emas!)

Belgi so'zlar: but, however, actually, in fact, on second thoughts — bulardan KEYIN to'g'ri javob keladi.

⏰ 15:00 — POST 3
🎯 Strategiya: paraphrase

Audioda savoldagi aynan o'sha so'zlar EMAS, sinonim ishlatiladi:
Savol: "expensive" → Audio: "costs a lot / pricey"
Shuning uchun MA'NONI tinglang, faqat so'zni emas. Variantlarni eshitilish tartibi bo'yicha emas, MA'NOSI bo'yicha belgilang.
