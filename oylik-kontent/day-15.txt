🗣 Part 3: chuqur muhokama

Part 3'da Part 2 mavzusi bo'yicha ABSTRAKT, umumiy savollar beriladi ("Why do people...?", "How will... change?"). Bu yerda fikr, sabab va misol talab qilinadi.

Maslahat: javobni kengaytiring — fikr + sabab + misol + qarama-qarshi tomon.

⏰ 12:00 — POST 2
💬 Fikr bildirish iboralari

• I would argue that ... — Men shunday deb hisoblayman ...
• It seems to me that ... — Menimcha ...
• On the one hand ... on the other hand ... — Bir tomondan ... boshqa tomondan ...
• That said, ... — Shunga qaramay, ...
• There's no doubt that ... — Shubhasiz ...

⏰ 15:00 — POST 3
🎯 Misol javob

❓ "Why do some people prefer to live in cities?"
✅ "Well, I'd argue the main reason is opportunity. Cities offer better jobs and education. For example, most universities are in big cities. That said, some people find them too crowded and stressful."

Struktura: fikr → sabab → misol → qarama-qarshi.
