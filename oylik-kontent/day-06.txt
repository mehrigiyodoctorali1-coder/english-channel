🔧 Present Perfect vs Past Simple

Eng ko'p adashtiriladigan grammatika:
• Past Simple — TUGAGAN vaqt, aniq payt: yesterday, in 2020, last week.
   "I visited London in 2019."
• Present Perfect — natijasi HOZIR muhim yoki vaqt noaniq: ever, never, already, yet, since, for.
   "I have visited London." (qachonligi muhim emas)

⏰ 12:00 — POST 2
💬 Signal so'zlar (belgi beruvchi so'zlar)

Past Simple bilan: yesterday, ago, last year, in 2020, when I was young.
Present Perfect bilan: ever, never, just, already, yet, so far, recently, since 2020, for 5 years.

"since" = boshlanish nuqtasi (since Monday).
"for" = davomiylik (for 3 days).

⏰ 15:00 — POST 3
🎯 Misollarni solishtiring

❌ "I have seen him yesterday." (yesterday — tugagan vaqt!)
✅ "I saw him yesterday."
✅ "I have already seen that film." (vaqt noaniq, natija muhim)
✅ "She has lived here for ten years." (hali ham yashaydi)
✅ "She lived there for ten years." (endi yashamaydi)

⏰ 18:00 — POST 4
📝 Homework + Useful word

To'g'ri shaklni tanlang:
1. I ___ (finish) my homework. Can we go now?
2. We ___ (go) to Samarkand last summer.
3. He ___ (not / eat) sushi before.
(Javob: 1 have finished, 2 went, 3 has not eaten)

💡 Useful word: experience /ɪkˈspɪə.ri.əns/ — tajriba (Have you ever...? bilan ishlating)
