⏰ 09:00 — POST 1
🗣 Noma'lum mavzuда gapirish

Imtihonda bilmagan mavzu chiqsa — to'xtab qolmaslik muhim. "Vaqt yutuvchi" iboralar yordam beradi:
• That's an interesting question ... — Qiziq savol ...
• Let me think for a moment ... — Bir o'ylab ko'ray ...
• Well, it's hard to say, but ... — Aytish qiyin, lekin ...

Bu iboralar jimlikni to'ldiradi va tabiiy ko'rinadi.

⏰ 12:00 — POST 2
💬 Paraphrasing — so'z esga tushmasa

So'z yodingizdan chiqsa, BOSHQACHA tushuntiring:
• "It's a kind of ..." — Bu bir turdagi ...
• "It's the thing you use to ..." — Bu ... uchun ishlatadigan narsa
• "It's similar to ..." — Bu ... ga o'xshaydi

Misol: "thermometer" esingizда yo'q → "the thing you use to measure temperature".

⏰ 15:00 — POST 3
🎯 Linking words (Speaking uchun)

Tabiiy bog'lovchilar ravonlik beradi:
• and then, after that — keyin
• because, so — chunki, shuning uchun
• but, although — lekin, garchi
• for example, like — masalan
⚠️ Yozma bog'lovchilarni (moreover, furthermore) Speaking'da kam ishlating — sun'iy eshitiladi.

📝 Homework + Useful word
