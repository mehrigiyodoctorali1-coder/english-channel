⏰ 09:00 — POST 1
📖 True / False / Not Given

Bu eng qiyin savol turi. Ma'nosi:
• TRUE — gap matndagi ma'lumotга MOS keladi
• FALSE — gap matnga ZID (qarama-qarshi)
• NOT GIVEN — matnda bu haqda HECH narsa yo'q

Eng katta xato: o'z bilimingizga tayanish. Faqat MATNGA qarang.

⏰ 12:00 — POST 2
💬 FALSE va NOT GIVEN farqi

• FALSE — matn TESKARISINI aytadi.
   Gap: "The museum is free." Matn: "Entry costs $5." → FALSE
• NOT GIVEN — matn bu haqda gapirmaydi.
   Gap: "The museum is popular." Matn narx haqida gapiradi, mashhurlik haqida emas → NOT GIVEN

Ma'lumot YO'Q bo'lsa = NOT GIVEN.

⏰ 15:00 — POST 3
🎯 Strategiya

1. Gapdagi kalit so'zni belgilang.
2. Matndan o'sha qismni scan qiling.
3. Gapni matn bilan diqqat bilan solishtiring.
4. "all, always, never, only" kabi mutlaq so'zlarga ehtiyot bo'ling — ko'pincha FALSE bo'ladi.
5. Savollar matn tartibida keladi.

⏰ 18:00 — POST 4
📝 Homework + Useful word

Matn: "Tea was first grown in China. Today it is popular worldwide."
Aniqlang (T/F/NG):
1. Tea originated in China. →
2. Tea is only drunk in Asia. →
3. Tea is the healthiest drink. →
(Javob: 1 True, 2 False, 3 Not Given)
